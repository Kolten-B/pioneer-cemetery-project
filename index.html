Many individuals buried in Pioneer Cemetery served in the military. As we continue improving this site, we’ll highlight and honor veteran graves while respecting every person’s story.

Data, Privacy, and Integrity

These records are based on public cemetery information provided by the City of Rexburg. No sensitive personal information (such as addresses or Social Security numbers) is stored.

Future versions of this site will allow family members to suggest updates or add biographies and photos. Those changes will go through an admin review step before appearing publicly. This helps protect the integrity of the records and prevents vandalism or inaccurate edits.
